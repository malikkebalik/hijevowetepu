Ed Fry

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Ed Fry was born in Chicago, Illinois but raised in Texas, where he graduated from Trinity University. Ed made his television debut on the soap opera "Another World". He has also appeared in series such as "Chappelle's Show", "Sex and The City", and "All My Children"> before landing the role of Larry McDermott on "As The World Turns".

In addition to acting Ed is a community and health activist. Ed donates his time to the Muscular Dystrophy Association, the Sarcoma Foundation of America and God's Love We Deliver.
