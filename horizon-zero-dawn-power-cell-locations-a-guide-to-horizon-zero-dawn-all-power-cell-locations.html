Horizon Zero Dawn

Horizon Zero Dawn is a 2017 action role-playing game developed by Guerrilla Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Aloy, a teenage hunter, sets out to find her past in a world overrun by machines. Ranged weapons, a spear, and stealth are required to combat mechanical monsters and other hostile soldiers. A skill tree allows the player to obtain new abilities and benefits. Side quests and free exploration are available to the player. It was released for PlayStation 4 in 2017 and Microsoft Windows in 2020.

Horizon Zero Dawn Power Cell Locations

Power Cells are one-of-a-kind and difficult-to-find artifacts required to accomplish the Ancient Armory side quest in Horizon: Zero Dawn. You'll acquire a powerful Shield Weaver armor after collecting all 5 hidden Power Cells, which makes Aloy practically impenetrable for a fixed amount of time while wearing it. We'll walk you through finding all 5 secret Power Cells that have been placed across the area in our Power Cells Locations guide.

Below are the power cells in the sequence in which they can be earned during the main questline.

Power Cell 01: Underground Bunker

After completing the prologue as Young Aloy, the first Power Cell can be found while exploring the open world. Retrace your steps through the barracks and down the stairs until you reach a closed door in the ruins where you found the Focus gadget.

To return to the second floor, pry open the door ahead of you and proceed through the offices. At the top of the stairs, you'll find a room on your right with a doorway covered by rock formations. Smash the boulders with your spear and grab the power cell from the desk.

Power Cell 02: All-Mother Temple

Following The Proving, the next Power Cell may be located in Mother's Watch's All-Mother Temple during the mission The Womb of the Mountain. Following Matriarch Teersa out of the fortress after discovering your equipment and conversing with her, traverse the hall to the closed door.

There is an open ventilation duct next to the locked door; enter the room beyond the door and gather a Power Cell by moving through it. The gates to the All-Mother Temple will be temporarily closed after this task is completed. The doors may be locked if you return after finishing The Womb of the Mountain. Don't worry, it'll only be for a short period of time. During the main storyline, Mother's Watch will reopen.

There's no need to get back in because you won't be able to obtain all five Power Cells before The Mountain That Fell anyhow — but if you're feeling adventurous and don't want to do it the old-fashioned way, there is a way to break through the wall borders at the back of the temple while mounted.

Simply return as part of The Heart of the Nora objective to re-enter the All-Mother Temple. You won't be able to fast-travel directly to the temple (for reasons you'll soon see), so choose the closest campfire and walk there.

Power Cell 03: Maker’s End

The third power cell may be found in Maker's End, which was first discovered during the same-named story quest. You'll want to mount the tower at the east end of the office to the very tip-top of the skyscraper once you've scaled the F.A.S. building all the way up to Faro's Office, which is near the mission's end. Power Cell 04: The Grave Hoard

Starting with the quest The Grave Hoard, you can collect the next Cell.To finish the quest, you'll need to open an electronic lock by resetting three systems after descending beneath the ruins. After you've opened the lock, look on the other side of the door for this power cell, which is laying on the ground.

Power Cell 05: Gaia Prime

During The Mountain That Fell, the final power cell is discovered amid the ruins.

If you try to enter GAIA before this task, you will be able to take out all of the major adversaries and make your way to the door, but you will be unable to do so since vital climbing steps are missing, which will probably be added throughout the main plot.

Climb the yellow ladder to enter Sylens' workshop once you arrive here as part of the mission. Speak with Sylens before descending the elevator shaft and rappelling into the ruins.

Turn left and go north before continuing deeper into the mountain and following your objective marker to the right. Upon the left side of the rock, you'll notice a distinct colored section. Climb up the rubble in front of you and into the blue-lit cave. The final Power Cell is kept on a shelf in the rear room. Once you've collected all five Power Cells, head to the Ancient Armory to receive your gift by solving a series of puzzles.
