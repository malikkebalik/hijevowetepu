Carly Pearce

Publish date: 2024-09-25

Carly Pearce is an American country music singer and songwriter who rose to prominence after her debut single, “Every Little Thing,” was released in 2017. Pearce has also gained notoriety through performing alongside well-known country acts like as Florida Georgia Line, Gary Allan, and Hunter Hayes. For her popular track, “Every Little Thing,” Pearce earned a CMT Music Award for “Breakthrough Video of the Year” and a Radio Disney Music Award for “The Freshest!-Radio Disney Country Best New Artist” in 2018.
