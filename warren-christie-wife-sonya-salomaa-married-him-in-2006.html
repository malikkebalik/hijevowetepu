Warren Christie Wife Sonya Salomaa Married Him In 2006

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Warren Christie wife Sonya Salomaa is well known for her acting in The Collector and Durham County. Sonya has won two Leo Awards and nominated for Gemini Awards.

Salomaa was born in 1974 in Sudbury, Canada. She was then raised in Prince George, British Columbia. For her education, she studied Bachelor of Science in Forestry at the University of Northern British Columbia.

Further, she does par time job mapping, research, and engineering in many fields and organizations.

In 2000 Sonya first appeared on the screen for the role in the TV movie 2gother. She had the role of Maya Kandinski in the Canadian drama series name The Collector in 2005. She won Leo Awards for Best Actress in a supporting role. 

The actress has also been a guest appearance in many TV shows such as Covert Affairs, Flashpoint, and Supernatural. In 2010, she was one of the presenters for the Leo Awards.

Sonya has played in many films such as House of the Dead, Firelight, Fatal Reunion, Black Eyed Dog, Watchmen, Cut Bank, and others.

Additionally, she has also been in TV shows like Pasadena, Dark Angel, Glory Dyas, Dead Like Me, Ties That Bind, Endgame, Broken Trust, and so on. 

For her outstanding performances, she has been rewarded and nominated. She has been awarded the Best Supporting Performance by a Female in a Dramatic Series by Leo Awards for continuously two years i.e. 2005 and 2006.

Moreover, she has also been nominated for Leo Awards and Gemini Awards for the Best supporting performance and the best lead performance.
