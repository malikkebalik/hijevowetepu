Chrissie Fit Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Bio:
Chrissie Fit (born Chrissie Marie Fit April 3, 1984 in Miami, Florida) is an American actress with an incredible singing voice. At 5 years old she began singing, and often performed for family and friends at parties while she was growing up. Chrissie began acting at 10, and performed in a number of school plays and musicals before finally landing her role on General Hospital in 2007. In 2013 she landed her breakout role as Chee Chee in Teen Beach Movie, and later appeared in the 2015 sequel, Teen Beach 2. In between those films, Chrissie joined the cast of Pitch Perfect as Flo, one of the new Barden Bellas in the film’s sequel. Aside from acting and singing, Chrissie also wrote, directed, and starred in a web series called The Subpranos.

Best Known For:
Chrissie is best known for her roles as Chee-Chee in Teen Beach Movie, and Flo in Pitch Perfect 2.

Personal Life:
Chrissie is rumored to have dated her Teen Beach co-star, John DeLuca.
