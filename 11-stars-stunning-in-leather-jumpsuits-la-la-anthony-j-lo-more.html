11 Stars Stunning In Leather Jumpsuits: La La Anthony, J.Lo &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-08-10

From Versace to Off-White, these celebrities rocked all kinds of leather jumpsuits to hit the town or the stage. We're taking a look at these stars who owned the look with a confidence all their own!

One of the hottest looks stars are rocking on the stage and the pavement is the leather jumpsuit. As winter winds down and spring is slowly emerging from the cold temperatures, leather jumpsuits are the perfect way to dress for the inconsistent weather. A versatile look that could easily be worn for a night on the town or a special occasion, these celebs are proving that leather jumpsuits look good anytime and anywhere.

From La La Anthony to Gigi Hadid, we’ve rounded up many famous fashionistas in a wide range of designers (from Off-White to Versace). We broke down the looks of a few of our favorites below, but you can check out all 14 celebrities rocking leather jumpsuits in the gallery attached above.

La La Anthony

La La Anthony strutted her stuff and posed up a storm while attending an event for Girls Inc. and The Police Athletic League at Gauchos Gym in the Bronx. The TV personality, 38, positively stunned in her monochromatic look. The black leather jumpsuit featured a plunging zipper neckline with pockets on the top section of the jumpsuit and a wrap belt around her waist. La La added some small accessories to her gorgeous hair and did her makeup to bring out her natural beauty. It was such a stunning look!

Jennifer Lopez

Leather jumpsuits aren’t just meant for hitting the pavement. They’re also for stages, like when superstar Jennifer Lopez wore one designed by Versace for her dazzling performance at the 2020 Super Bowl Halftime Show.

The “On The Floor” singer, 51, couldn’t have looked better in the designer leather jumpsuit that she wore to open her segment of the show, which featured beading throughout and cutouts around her torso and backside. J. Lo’s blown-out hair cascaded over her shoulders as she gave fans a performance — and look — they would never forget.

Kim Kardashian

Leather jumpsuits aren’t limited to the typical style you see biker chicks wearing on their motorcycles. They come in all cuts and materials, which Kim Kardashian proved in this brown PVC leather piece designed by Mowalola Ogunlesi. The top half of the jumpsuit was designed like an inverted V-shaped corset that connected to wide culottes, which emphasized the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star’s famous hourglass figure. Kim wore the wild west look while visiting the Wyoming estate of her then-husband, Kanye West, in June of 2020; she went on to file for divorce in Feb. 2021.

Ciara

Ciara took a cue from the ’80s in this leather jumpsuit designed by Michael Costello. Designed with structured, padded shoulders and a skintight fit, we couldn’t help but think of similar jumpsuits that pop stars rocked in retro music videos. Instead of the set of a MTV shoot, though, Ciara was wearing this designer look for the 5th Annual Hollywood Beauty Awards in Los Angeles on Feb. 17, 2019. She looked gorgeous, per usual!

Olivia Culpo 

One celeb who has continued to show how absolutely effortless their style is happens to be Olivia Culpo! The former pageant queen, 27, showed off her forest green leather jumpsuit while attending an event for Sports Illustrated on May 10, 2019. Olivia looked so incredibly confident in the ensemble, which featured appliqués atop her straps for extra detail and definition. The brunette beauty made the jumpsuit the centerpiece of her look, pulling he hair up in a bun and accessorizing with simple hoop earrings, snakeskin heels, and a small purse.

Gigi Hadid

Gigi Hadid looked like she time-traveled to the future instead of Paris in this Off-White jumpsuit, which she wore amid Paris Fashion Week in March of 2019. It’s rare to see a white leather jumpsuit: and even rarer to see one designed with color block gloves. We expect nothing less from the model whose streetwear ensembles are just as visionary as they are on the runways she walks!
