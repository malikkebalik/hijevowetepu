Thalia's About

[✎]

Thalia was born on August 26, 1971 (age 52) in Mexico City, Mexico. She is a Celebrity World Music Singer. Mexican singer who was recognized as the most successful female solo Mexican artist internationally by media conglomerates. She has won many honors, including four Latin Billboard awards. She has been a member of the band Timbiriche (band).  According to numerology, Thalia's Life Path Number is 7. She joined tvshows named Marimar (1994), María la del Barrio (1995 – 1996), Rosalinda (1999), María Mercedes (1992 – 1993) and Quinceañera (1987 – 1988). Her successful albums are Valiente, Primera Fila, Latina, Arrasando, Love. Her popular songs are No Me AcuerdoValiente · 2018, Desde Esa NocheLatina · 2016 and Estoy EnamoradoPrimera Fila · 2009. She had 2 children Sabrina Sakaë Mottola Sodi, Matthew Alejandro Mottola Sodi. Her spouse is Tommy Mottola (m. 2000). Her height is 5ft 4 ½ (164cm). More information on Thalia can be found here. Thalia Before Fame

[✎]

Thalia, born 8-26-1971, before achieving notoriety as a World Music Singer, she appeared in her first television commercial in Mexico at a year old and later at four years old, she began taking ballet classes at the National Conservatory of Music of Mexico.

Success of Thalia

[✎]

As an accomplished World Music Singer, she has been an official celebrity ambassador for March of Dimes since 2004. In 2019, she released the hit single "No Me Acuerdo" featuring Natti Natasha (World Music Singer) and the music video for the song has accumulated more than 1 billion views on YouTube.
