UPDATE: Cassia County Sheriff's Office say power is restored to homes in Burley

Publish date: 2024-09-09

BURLEY, Idaho (CBS 2) — Cassia County Sheriff's Office updated their previous social media post to say that the City of Burley had fixed the power issue.

The post added that, if a Burley resident still didn't have power, they should contact the sheriff's office.

In the initial post, the power outage was blamed on problems at the substation.
