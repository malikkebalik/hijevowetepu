Louis William "Bill" Conradt, Jr. (January 30, 1950 – November 5, 2006) was an assistant district attorney from Texas. He became inextricably linked to Dateline NBC's To Catch a Predator, a TV series which conducted sting operations against suspected sexual predators that target children online. Local law enforcement conducted a sting operation that identified Conradt as a suspect and Dateline cameras recorded the events that followed. Conradt fatally shot himself upon encountering SWAT team members that were serving an arrest warrant at his home. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
