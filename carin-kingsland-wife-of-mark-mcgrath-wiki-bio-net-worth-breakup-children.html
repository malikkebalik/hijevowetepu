Upbringing & Early Career

Carin Kingsland was born on the 15th of October 1973 in California, USA. Carin, who comes from Mexican descent, was raised in Rancho Cucamonga which is close to the Sierra Nevada Mountains. Carin was interested in cosmetics from a young age and was involved with the Cosmetology Career Center, during her high school years. After graduating from high school, Carin enrolled in the beautician course and received her license. Carin later moved to Los Angeles in search of better opportunities and started working as a professional beautician in her neighborhood.

Meeting Mark and Marriage

Carin and Mark first met at The Viper Room in 1994. Thinking about the time the pair first met, Mike, revealed that he said, ‘I Love You’ to Carin without even getting to know her. The pair began dating soon after and faced a series of hardships during the early years of the relationship. However, both Mark and Carin persevered and went on to date for 18 years before tying the knot. They walked down the aisle in a private wedding ceremony on September 24, 2012, in Southern California attended by their close friends and family. Mark revealed the news to People Magazine following the marriage, recounting all the moments of happiness and despair the pair spent together.

“After 18 years of breakups, makeups, beautiful twins and an unwavering love that provided some pretty damn good song lyrics, we are happy to announce that we are finally married.”

A Loving Wife and Mother

Carin has twins with Mark and the pair are absolutely in love with their kids. Lydon and Harley were born in 2010, just four months into the pair’s engagement. As the pair struggled to get pregnant during their early years, Mark opened up that the pair had to depend on IVF.

“I tried my hardest the conventional way, but that didn’t work out for us. We did the IVF thing. It was intense and expensive, but we did it in one shot and, boom, here they are. My job now is to be the best parent I can be because that is all that matters.”

While Mark mentioned that he had a lot to learn about parenting, he was full of praise for Carin who was already the perfect mother. Following the announcement of the news, Mark gave an interview to Daily News saying how his kids would be the luckiest in the world.

A Quick Write on her Husband-Mark McGrath

Mark McGrath is a versatile and talented American singer, composer and television host. He was born on March 15, 1968, in Hartford, Connecticut, and moved to Newport Beach, California when he was eight years old. He graduated from the University of Southern California with a degree in communications in 1990.

He is best known as the lead vocalist of the rock band Sugar Ray, which he formed with his friends in 1992. The band achieved mainstream success with their hit song “Fly” in 1997, which topped the Billboard Hot 100 chart for four weeks. McGrath also wrote or co-wrote many of the band’s other popular songs, such as “Every Morning”, “Someday” and “When It’s Over”. 

Besides his musical career, McGrath has also ventured into television and film. He has appeared as himself or as a host in various shows, such as Rock & Roll Jeopardy!, Extra, Don’t Forget the Lyrics!, Killer Karaoke and The Office. He has also acted in movies like Scooby-Doo, Wild Things and The Sweetest Thing.
