Natasha Negovanlis is a Canadian actress, writer, and producer who is best known for her role as Mircalla “Carmilla” Karnstein in the web series, Carmilla. She has also played a wide range of characters in other shows and movies like Victoria in Darken, Ellia Henderson in Haunted or Hoax, Cassie in Almost Adults, and Claire Ganski in CLAIREvoyant. Moreover, Natasha Negovanlis has earned a decent social media fan base with more than 200k followers on Instagram and more than 150k followers on Twitter.
