Here is an insight into the biography, age, net worth, wiki, family, and latest updates about Lauren Kettering.

Lauren Kettering, born on April 4, 2003, is a well-known American TikTok star and social media influencer. She gained fame for her lip-syncing and dancing videos on TikTok, ultimately leading to her appearance in the Brat series, Attaway General, in 2022.

Her charming face, adorable smile, impressive dancing skills, and appealing physique have won her a dedicated fan base across various social media platforms. With over 1.2 million Instagram followers and 6.4 million TikTok followers, she has garnered substantial popularity.
