Who Are Aleksandar Kovacevic’s Parents?

Aleksandar Kovacevic was born in New York, United States of America to his parents Milan Kovacevic (Father) and Milanka Kovacevic (Mother).

Aleksandar’s father Milan originally belonged to the Serbian capital Belgrade. Later he relocated to the United States of America to study computer science at the UCLA (University of California, Los Angeles) college.

Aleksandar’s mother Milanka is originally from Travnik, Town in Bosnia and Herzegovina. She is best known for the depiction of Ivo Andric – the Serbian Nobel Prize in Literature winner – in his novel Travnička Veronika (The Days of the Consuls).

Milan and Milanka both were almost professional TT (table tennis) players and met on the junior circuit before coming into a relationship and spending life together in the United States of America.
