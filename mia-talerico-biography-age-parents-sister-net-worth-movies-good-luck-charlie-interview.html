Mia Talerico Biography

Mia Talerico is an American actress best known for her appearance in Disney Channel’s Good Luck Charlie. She started acting when she was eleven months old 

Mia Talerico Age

Talerico was born on 17 September 2008 in Santa Barbara, California, United States. She is 10 years old as of 2018

Mia Talerico Family | Mia Talerico Parents | Mia Talerico Sister

Jason Dolley: Mia Talerico is the Best Baby Ever

Source: justjaredjr.com

Jason Dolley stars as PJ Duncan in the new series, Good Luck Charlie, premiering on Disney Channel THIS Sunday.

JustJaredJr.com got a chance to catch up with the 18-year-old actor to chat about his costars, his character, and why fans should tune into the premiere episode. Check it:

On working with “Charlie” Mia Talerico: “Mia is amazing. She is hands down the greatest television baby ever seen or heard of. It’s almost like she reads the script. We’ll get into a scene and whatever is on the script that she is supposed to do, she does it. It’s kind of freaky (laughs). She’s just adorable and loves everyone on the set. She’ll come down the hallway on set and everyone will stop and say hi to her and she waves. She’s so cute and awesome to work with.”

On his character, PJ: “He’s the oldest of the family. PJ is a wannabe rockstar; he loves playing his guitar which he’s not very good at. He plays in a basement band with his buddy who plays drums. They play loud and hard. PJ is a good guy; he’s not dumb but he’s certainly not the smartest of the family either.”

On the appeal of the new series: “When I read the script for the first time, it just had this authentic feel to it and had a Full House kind of feel. When I read it, I was like, ‘Wow, this is for Disney Channel. This is something that families can sit down together and watch. Everybody would laugh and enjoy it and want to watch the next one.’ I think that’s what is really unique about the show and what is really cool about it.”
